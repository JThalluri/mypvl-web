Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Analytics Cookies

    Microsoft Clarity: Helps us understand how users interact with our website to improve user experience.

  • Security Cookies

    Google reCAPTCHA: Protects our forms from spam and abuse while ensuring real human interaction.

  • Functional Cookies

    tawk.to: Enables live chat functionality and remembers your chat session preferences.

  • Form Cookies

    Formspree: Manages form submissions and maintains form state during your session.

Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent.

Essential Cookies

Some cookies are essential for the website to function properly. Disabling them may affect your user experience.

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.
